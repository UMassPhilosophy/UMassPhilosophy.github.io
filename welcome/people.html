People

Here is a list of people you should know about if you don’t already, as well as some information about who is the best person to contact depending on what kind of issues you need help with.

  • Kevin Klement (klement@umass.edu), Department Chair

    The chair is heavily involved in the day-to-day administration of the Department, as well as its long term planning. The chair is also responsible for most decisions involving finances, including TA/TO appointments. The chair can answer questions regarding such appointments and other issues involving the department as a whole.

  • Alejandro Pérez Carballo (apc@umass.edu), Graduate Program Director (GPD)

    The GPD is responsible for overseeing the PhD program and admissions to it. The GPD can answer questions concerning degree requirements, including required courses, starred papers, etc. and is also happy to discuss any other issues related to the graduate program, problems relating to it or your ability to succeed in it.

  • Beth Grybko (bellena@philos.umass.edu), Business Manager and Graduate Program Assistant

    Beth is the main administrative person in the department, and of the graduate program in particular. Beth can answer questions concerning how one receives funding, how to pick up office keys, your academic record, how to reserve rooms on campus, and so on.

  • Darlene Gore (darlenegore@umass.edu), Undergraduate Program Assistant

    Darlene can answer questions concerning undergraduate requirements, resources for undergraduate students, undergraduate grading, including incompletes, enrollment overrides, the web site, copier accounts and using the copier, etc. In general, if you have a question that concerns you as a graduate student, employee or fellow, ask Beth. If it concerns your role as TA or TO responsible for undergraduate students, ask Darlene.
